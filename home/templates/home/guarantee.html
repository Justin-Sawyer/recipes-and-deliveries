Recipes and Deliveries Guarantee

Track and Trace

All RecipesandDeliveries products are fully traceable, from Red Tractor certified suppliers for meat and vegetables to Marine Stewardship Council suppliers for fish.

Cold Chain

All RecipesandDeliveries Recipe Kits are delivered with Heat Sensitive labels, so you can immediately see that the cold chain has been respected. It's why we ask you to open your delivery when you receive it: If the sticker shows that the cold chain has been broken, simply reject the delivery!

Suppliers

Where possible, only local suppliers are used, for RecipesandDeliveries Recipe Kits, from Red Tractor certified fresh produce to locally grown herbs.

Environment

Local produce means less resources are used, thus protecting both our economy and our environment. RecipesandDeliveries takes its environmental responisibility seriously.

Packaging

Where possible, all RecipesandDeliveries Recipe Kits packaging is recyclable. Where cool boxes or freezer boxes are used, you are asked to pay a minimal deposit on the box: When you return the box for re-use, your deposit is returned, thus ensuring less plastic is used. This deposit is included in your first order.

Delivery

All deliveries within 24 hours, guaranteed.
